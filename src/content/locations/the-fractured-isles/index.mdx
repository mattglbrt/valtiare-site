The Fractured Isles were once the country of **Manira**. Manira shared its
southern border with Ruhar and its northern border with Emuin.

When Manira fractured, it shattered into a series of floating isles held
together by a magical energy. It is now called the Fractured Isles.

The cause of the Fracturing has not been revealed.
