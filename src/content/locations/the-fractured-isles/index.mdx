The Fractured Isles were once the country of **Manira**, set between Ruhar
to the south and Emuin to the north.

When Manira fractured, it broke apart into a cluster of floating islands,
held up by some kind of magic that no one has explained.

The cause of the Fracturing has not been revealed.
