The forests of Ruhar stretch further than any map has yet measured. Travelers
speak of oaks with faces worn into the bark, of streams that change direction
after nightfall, and of settlements so deep within the trees that the outside
world has become a rumor to them.

The Svirfneblin keep old halls beneath its roots. Pilgrims and exiles walk its
paths. The wounded are carried here more often than the wounded ever expect to
be carried anywhere — the forest has a way of finding them.
