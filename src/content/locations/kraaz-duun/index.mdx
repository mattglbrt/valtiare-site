Kraaz'Duun is a Svirfneblin stronghold carved into the shimmering caverns
far beneath what was once Manira. Its people lived their lives among
crystal-lit halls, winding tunnels, and the ever-present chorus of
murmuring stone. Most deep gnomes felt an unbreakable bond with the deep
places of the world, trusting in shadow, silence, and ancient earth-magic.

When the Fracturing of Manira split the earth, it tore open a jagged
crevasse in the heart of Kraaz'Duun, plunging upward toward the surface.
Through that wound a column of true, golden sunlight poured down into
the stronghold. The people of Kraaz'Duun recoiled, fearful of the unknown
radiance — all but the young outlier Rollo, who stepped forward into it
and climbed up into the light.
