Kraaz'Duun is a Svirfneblin stronghold carved into the caverns far beneath
what was once Manira. Its people lived among crystal-lit halls and winding
tunnels, with the low murmur of the stone always around them. Most deep
gnomes felt bound to the underground, and trusted shadow, silence, and old
earth-magic.

When the Fracturing split the earth, it tore a jagged crevasse through the
heart of Kraaz'Duun, opening upward toward the surface. Golden sunlight
poured down into the stronghold. The people recoiled, afraid of the unknown
light. All but a young outlier named Rollo, who stepped forward into it and
climbed up out of the dark.
