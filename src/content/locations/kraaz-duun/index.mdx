For most of its existence, Kraaz'Duun was a place of long, patient quiet. The
Svirfneblin who called it home trusted in shadow, silence, and ancient
earth-magic, and kept their lives among the crystal-lit halls and the
murmuring stone. The outside world was a rumor and, to most of them, a
threat best left unexamined.

Then the Fracturing split the ceiling of the central hall and a column of
true sunlight fell into Kraaz'Duun for the first time in recorded memory. The
stronghold has not been the same since. Some of its people turned away from
the light. Some studied it. A few, most famously the young outlier Rollo,
climbed up into it and did not come back.
