This is a map of **Vaelloss**, the world.

Three of its countries have begun to have stories told about them:
**Manira**, **Ruhar**, and **Emuin**.

Manira shattered into a broken archipelago of floating isles held
together by a magical energy. It is now called the **Fractured Isles**.
**Ruhar**, to its south, answered by becoming overgrown with flora and
fauna. **Emuin**, to its north, suffered the opposite fate: its land
and its people became gaunt and drained of life.

The cause of the Fracturing has not been revealed.

The other countries on the map (Glaice, the Fist, Algard, Kallhamar,
Thean, Tanion, Myr, Esnor, and the Spire) do not yet have stories of
their own.
