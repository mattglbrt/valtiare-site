**Echoes of Ash** collects the short fictions that grew out of an ongoing tabletop
campaign set on Vaelloss during its long cooling. The arc follows a scattered cast of
hearthkeepers, wanderers, and unwelcome couriers — none of whom share a table, and
most of whom never meet — bound together by the same dimming sky and the same
stubborn refusal to let certain things be forgotten.

The gameplay that produced these stories is kept out of the telling. What remains is
only what the characters would have remembered afterward: a confession, a letter, a
candle set on a windowsill for a reason no one else was told.
