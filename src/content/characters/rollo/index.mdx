Rollo is small, even by svirfneblin standards, and carries himself like
someone who has not yet decided whether the world is a thing to be afraid of
or a thing to be marveled at. He walks beside Thorek on the forest roads, a
half-step behind and a half-step wide-eyed, stopping often to stare upward
at things the dwarf has long since stopped noticing.

How the two of them came to be traveling together is his own story, yet to
be told.
