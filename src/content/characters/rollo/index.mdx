Rollo was born in the stone-buried stronghold of Kraaz'Duun, a remote
Svirfneblin settlement carved into shimmering caverns far beneath the
surface of what was once Manira. His people trusted in shadow, silence,
and ancient earth-magic. Rollo, almost alone among them, yearned for
the light above.

When the Fracturing of Manira split the earth, a jagged crevasse tore
open in the heart of Kraaz'Duun and true sunlight poured down through
it for the first time in living memory. Rollo stepped forward into the
warmth rather than recoiling. He traded nearly everything he owned for
an iron sword and a shield, climbed toward the crevasse, and did not
look back.

He now walks the Forest of Ruhar, waiting for a quest worthy of his
heart.
