Rollo was born in Kraaz'Duun, a Svirfneblin stronghold carved deep into the
shimmering caverns beneath Manira. His people trusted in shadow, silence,
and ancient earth-magic. Rollo, almost alone among them, trusted instead in
a light he had never seen.

From childhood he imagined the world above — open sky, wind, clarity,
courage, the half-remembered things surface stories whispered about. He
crafted shields from rotten wood and practice-blades from polished stones
and held to a code that came from somewhere he could not name. His elders
thought him eccentric. His peers thought him strange. He kept practicing
anyway.

When the Fracturing split the earth and a column of true sunlight poured
into Kraaz'Duun, Rollo was the only one who stepped forward rather than
back. He traded nearly everything he owned for an iron sword and a shield
and climbed toward the light.

Now he walks the Forest of Ruhar, waiting for a quest worthy of his heart.
He travels often at Thorek Duskworn's side. He has not yet forgotten what
he came up here for.
