Triste is a young phase elf whose presence always seems to drift a
half-step ahead of anyone trying to understand her. She moves like
moonlight slipping through branches, soft in voice and impossible to
pin down. Her people walk the thin places of the world, and Triste
carries that same quiet strangeness, as if she is listening to things
no one else can hear.

Curiosity rules her life. She seeks experience for its own sake, not
gold or renown. Old ruins, whispered legends, forgotten relics, and
reckless journeys appeal to her far more than comfort or safety. For
reasons she has never explained, she often chooses to bring a
companion along. That companion is Garrin Vosk.

Her affection is gentle and constant. Her inner thoughts stay
carefully veiled.
