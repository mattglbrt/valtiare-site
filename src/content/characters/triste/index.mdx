Triste moves through the world a half-step ahead of anyone trying to
understand her. She is young by her people's reckoning, a phase elf whose kind
walk the thin places — the edges where one reality leans against another. She
speaks softly, smiles often, and seems to be listening to things no one else
can hear.

Curiosity is her engine. Old ruins, forgotten relics, reckless journeys — she
chases the experiences themselves rather than any reward they promise. She has
a habit of pulling travel companions into danger with nothing more than a
smile and a spark in her eye. The grizzled hunter Garrin Vosk walks at her
side now, drawn in first by mystery and kept there, as far as anyone can
tell, by something softer he has not said out loud.

What she is actually seeking, she rarely reveals. Her affection is gentle
and constant. Her inner thoughts stay carefully veiled.
