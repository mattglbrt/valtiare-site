The mage calls himself Eloryn Vale, though few know whether that is his
true name or simply the one he offers to strangers. He carries the
reputation of a wandering scholar, soft-spoken and endlessly curious
about ruins, civilizations, and the ebb of history across the ages. To
most people he seems harmless, a man more interested in forgotten lore
than the raw practice of magic.

This version of himself is a lie he has cultivated with care.

Beneath the gentleness lies a mind consumed by hunger. Eloryn does not
seek knowledge for understanding, only for leverage. His staff, which
he calls a tool of study, is in truth an anchor for a ritual he has
been constructing for years. He intends to shed mortality itself and
take a place among the greater forces of creation, no matter the cost.
