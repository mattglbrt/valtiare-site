Barrow is Garrin Vosk's hound. He follows at Garrin's heel with easy
loyalty, ears flicking at every sound, nose always searching. He greets
Triste with a thump of his tail.

Garrin talks to Barrow more than he talks to anyone else. Some days
Barrow feels like the last proof that Garrin is still connected to the
world at all.
