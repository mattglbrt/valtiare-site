Saelvyn is the name the Orchard pilgrims use, because a traveler must be called
something. It is almost certainly not their name. Those who have tried to ask directly
report that the answer they were given slipped, afterward, out of memory — leaving
only the shape of the asking.

They wear a brass half-mask with two small slits above the eyes, and a cloak the color
of old smoke. They speak rarely, and always quietly, and always in a cadence that
feels borrowed from someone long dead.

What is known: Saelvyn tends the Keeper tree in the Glass Orchard. What is
suspected: they are tending it **for** someone, and have been for a very long time.
